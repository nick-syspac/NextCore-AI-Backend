About the Suite

The EduAI Compliance Suite is an integrated collection of AI-powered tools designed specifically for Australian Registered Training Organisations (RTOs). Each component addresses critical compliance requirements while streamlining operational workflows and improving educational outcomes.

Built on NextCore AI's advanced language models and designed with deep understanding of ASQA Standards, the suite ensures your RTO maintains continuous compliance while delivering exceptional training and assessment services.

All components integrate seamlessly with your existing Student Management System (SMS) and Learning Management System (LMS), providing real-time compliance monitoring, automated workflows, and comprehensive audit trails.
