User Guide: Converting Your TAS

Step 1: Initiate Conversion

Navigate to your TAS document and select the "Convert to 2025 Standards" option.

POST /api/tenants/{tenant}/tas/{tas_id}/convert-to-2025/

Body:
{
  "session_name": "ICT40120 TAS Conversion to 2025",
  "ai_model": "gpt-4o",
  "options": {
    "preserve_formatting": true,
    "update_terminology": true,
    "add_conversion_notes": true,
    "use_ai": true
  }
}

Conversion Options

  • preserve_formatting: Keep original document formatting (default: true)
  • update_terminology: Update clause ‚Üí standard, etc. (default: true)
  • add_conversion_notes: Add notes about conversion (default: true)
  • use_ai: Use AI for intelligent conversion (default: true)

Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the AI conversion?

The AI conversion service typically achieves 90-95% accuracy in standards mapping and content transformation. However, human review is always recommended to ensure context and RTO-specific details are correctly preserved.

Will my original TAS be modified?

No, your original TAS is never modified. The conversion service creates a new TAS document (version 2.0), leaving your 2015-compliant version intact.

How long does conversion take?

Most TAS documents convert in 2-5 minutes, depending on document size and complexity. Longer or more complex documents may take up to 10 minutes.

Can I convert multiple TAS documents at once?

Yes, you can initiate multiple conversion sessions. Each session runs independently and tracks its own progress.

What if the conversion fails?

If a conversion fails, the error is logged with details. You can retry the conversion or contact support. Your original TAS remains unaffected.

Can I edit the converted TAS?

Yes, after conversion, you can edit the new TAS document just like any other TAS. This allows you to refine AI-generated content and add RTO-specific details.
