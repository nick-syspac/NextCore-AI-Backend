üéØ What is Policy Comparator?

Policy Comparator is a specialized compliance tool within the NextCore AI platform designed to help Registered Training Organisations (RTOs) ensure their policies and procedures meet Australian Skills Quality Authority (ASQA) requirements.

Core Capabilities

  • Automated Policy Analysis: Upload or enter policy text for instant comparison
  • NLP-Based Matching: Advanced text similarity algorithms detect compliance coverage
  • Gap Identification: Automatically identifies missing requirements and compliance gaps
  • Threshold-Based Scoring: Configurable thresholds classify match quality (full, partial, weak, no match)
  • Actionable Recommendations: Specific suggestions to improve policy compliance
  • Batch Processing: Compare multiple policies against entire standards frameworks
  • Compliance Tracking: Historical tracking of policy compliance scores over time

üìã ASQA Compliance Context

The Australian Skills Quality Authority (ASQA) is the national regulator for Australia's vocational education and training sector. RTOs must comply with the Standards for Registered Training Organisations (RTOs) 2015.

Standards for RTOs 2015 - Coverage

Standard 1: Training and Assessment

Training and assessment delivered by the RTO meets the requirements of the training package

  • Clause 1.1: Training and assessment strategies
  • Clause 1.2: Industry currency
  • Clause 1.3-1.8: Assessment practices

Standard 2: Trainer and Assessor

Trainers and assessors are appropriately qualified and competent

  • Clause 2.1-2.2: Vocational competencies
  • Clause 2.3: Industry currency requirements
  • Clause 2.4: Training and assessment qualifications

Standard 3: Educational and Support Services

Physical and e-learning environment supports quality outcomes

  • Clause 3.1: Learning environment
  • Clause 3.2: Facilities and equipment
  • Clause 3.3: Learning and assessment resources

Standard 4: Certification

Accurate and timely certification of student outcomes

  • Clause 4.1: Issuance requirements
  • Clause 4.2: Unique Student Identifier
  • Clause 4.3: Certificate accuracy

Standard 5: Engagement with Employers

Effective engagement with industry and employers

  • Clause 5.1: Industry engagement strategies
  • Clause 5.2: Work placement arrangements
  • Clause 5.3: Consultation requirements

Standard 6: Complaints and Appeals

Fair and transparent complaints and appeals processes

  • Clause 6.1: Written procedures
  • Clause 6.2: Independence requirements
  • Clause 6.3-6.6: Process and timeframes

Standard 7: Governance and Administration

RTO has effective governance and administrative arrangements

  • Clause 7.1-7.3: Management systems
  • Clause 7.4: Student data accuracy
  • Clause 7.5: Marketing and advertising

Standard 8: Financial Management

Financial management ensures sustainability and student protection

  • Clause 8.1: Financial viability
  • Clause 8.2: Fee protection arrangements
  • Clause 8.3: Written agreements

Gap Analysis

Comprehensive gap detection with severity classification:

  • Critical Gaps: No match on "Critical" compliance level clauses
