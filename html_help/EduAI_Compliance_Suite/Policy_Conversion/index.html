User Guide: Converting Your Policies

Step 1: Initiate Conversion

Navigate to your policy document and select the "Convert to 2025 Standards" option.

POST /api/tenants/{tenant}/policies/{policy_id}/convert-to-2025/

Body:
{
  "session_name": "Assessment Policy Conversion to 2025",
  "ai_model": "gpt-4o",
  "options": {
    "preserve_formatting": true,
    "update_terminology": true,
    "add_conversion_notes": true,
    "use_ai": true
  }
}

Conversion Options

  • preserve_formatting: Keep original document formatting (default: true)
  • update_terminology: Update clause ‚Üí standard, etc. (default: true)
  • add_conversion_notes: Add notes about conversion (default: true)
  • use_ai: Use AI for intelligent conversion (default: true)

Batch Conversion

Converting Multiple Policies

For RTOs with extensive policy frameworks, the conversion service supports batch operations:

Recommended Approach:

  1. Prioritize: Start with critical policies (Assessment, Training Delivery, Complaints & Appeals)
  2. Group by Type: Convert policies of similar types together for consistency
  3. Review Incrementally: Review and approve each batch before proceeding
  4. Cross-Reference: Ensure converted policies align with each other
  5. Update Dependencies: Update references between policies

Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the AI conversion?

The AI conversion service typically achieves 90-95% accuracy in standards mapping and content transformation. However, human review is always recommended to ensure organizational-specific details and context are correctly preserved.

Will my original policy be modified?

No, your original policy is never modified. The conversion service creates a new policy document (with "-2025" suffix), leaving your 2015-compliant version intact.

How long does conversion take?

Most policy documents convert in 1-3 minutes, depending on document size and complexity. Very long or complex policies may take up to 5 minutes.

Can I convert multiple policies at once?

Yes, you can initiate multiple conversion sessions. Each session runs independently and tracks its own progress. We recommend converting policies in logical groups for easier review.

What if the conversion fails?

If a conversion fails, the error is logged with details. You can retry the conversion or contact support. Your original policy remains unaffected.

Can I edit the converted policy?

Yes, after conversion, you can edit the new policy document just like any other policy. This allows you to refine AI-generated content and add organizational-specific details.

What about policies not directly mapped to ASQA standards?

The service intelligently handles policies with indirect standards references. It will flag sections requiring manual review and provide recommendations for update approaches.
