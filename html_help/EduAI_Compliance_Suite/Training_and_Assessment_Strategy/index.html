ğŸ“‹ What is a TAS?

A Training and Assessment Strategy (TAS) is a mandatory document for Australian RTOs that outlines how training and assessment will be delivered for a specific qualification. It must demonstrate compliance with the ASQA Standards for RTOs 2015.

Key ASQA Requirements:

  • Clause 1.1: Training and assessment strategies meet training package requirements
  • Clause 1.3: Identification of learner characteristics and support needs
  • Clause 1.8: Valid, reliable, fair, and flexible assessment
  • Clauses 1.13-1.16: Trainer and assessor competency requirements
  • Clause 2.2: Continuous improvement processes
